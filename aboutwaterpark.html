Water Kingdom

Known as Asia's largest water theme park, Water Kingdom is the largest water park in India and the oldest known as well. The rides and the experience have only gone from good to great, all at a reasonable rate. Some old favourites remain, such as the artificial beach built inside the park. Not only this but there are also combined passes available to Essel World, right next door. Water Kingdom remains one of the most widely visited parks in India, from school picnics to even weddings and new year's Eve nights. It is the biggest water in India as well as Asia.

Timings: 10:00 AM – 06:00 PM

Price: Regular Ticket: Adult – INR 1250, Child – INR 825

Address: Global Pagoda Road, Gorai, Borivali West, Mumbai, Maharashtra

How to get there? Drive down to Mira-Bhayander road. It will take around 35–40 mins from Kashimira. The other route to the water park is to take MBBT Bus no 4 from Bhayander bus depot which takes you directly to the park.

Must try rides: What a coaster.

Website: www.waterkingdom.in

Contact number: +91 22 2845 2711

Adlabs Aquamagica

Stated by many as the best water park in India in a long time, Adlabs Aquamagica is most known for its polite staff and affordable food. Even though its prices are on the higher end of the range, a lot of fun is promised to those who go in groups, but on weekdays. Weekends see a huge influx of crowds from both crowded cities of Mumbai and Pune which ends up in long waits in the queue for each ride. Each ride has its own feel to it and the park has an excellent ambience overall. They have lots of seasonal discounts for students, couples and the like.

Timings: 10:00 AM – 06:00 PM

Price: Regular Ticket: Adult – INR 1250, Child – INR 825

Address: State Highway 92, Off Mumbai-Pune Expressway, Khalapur, Khopoli

How to get there? The water park is easily accessible from the nearby bus stations. Aquamagic provides a home to home pick and drops services that are available while booking tickets. You can pay an amount of INR 500 while ticketing.

Must try rides: Loopy Woopy

Website: www.imagicaaworld.com

Contact number: +91 22421304051

Ocean Park

Located 15km from Hyderabad City, Ocean Park welcomes a wide variety of water games and rides for all age groups. The key highlights of the place are the wave pool, aqua ride, splash and Tornado which are a few of the popular water rides. There are also rides including monkey rides, Mini Columbus, a tube slide and a toddler's pool for the kids. Dry rides like the super loop, bumping cars, bumper boats and ropeway are sure to keep you entertained. If you're hungry, there are also various cuisines within the ocean park premises.

Timings: 10:00 AM – 06:00 PM

Price: Regular Ticket: Adult – INR 500, Child – INR 400

Address: Near CBIT College, Shankerpally Road, Gandipet, Hyderabad, Telangana

How to get there? Hyderabad being a metropolitan city, you can reach the water park through public means of transport or government bus services. From Hyderabad city hire a local taxi to Mehadipatnam from her opt for a public bus directly to Ocean Park.

Must try rides: Tornado, Super Loop, and Mini Columbus.

Website: www.oceanparkhyderabad.com

Contact number: +91 22 2845 2711

Dreamworld Water Park

Set in the backdrop of the biggest waterfall in Kerala, Dreamworld amusement park gives you an exciting experience. The state-of-art facility boasts an 8ft deep wave pool, along with a raft ride which is over 30 ft. The park also houses 42 rides with more than half of them being water rides. The environment set in the middle of picturesque waterfalls makes it one of the best waterparks in the country. Rain dance, mermaid pool, multi-game complex for kids, video games, and a multitude of restaurants with ice cream parlours and buffet facilities are its main attractions.

Timings: 10:00 AM – 06:00 PM

Price: Regular Ticket: Adult – INR 700, Child – INR 700
Combine Ticket: Adult – INR 1000, Child – INR 675

Address: Athirappilly Road, Kanjirappilly P.O., Chalakudy, Thrissur

How to get there? It is situated 11 km from Chalakudy station. It is easily accessible by public transport or you can use a taxi or Auto.

Must try rides: water Ride

Website: www.dreamworldwaterpark.in

Contact number: +91 480 274 6935

MGM Dizzee World

Most commonly known as the Big on Fun, MGM Dizzee World is an amusement park in Chennai that entertains close to 1 Million visitors every year. In the entertainment sector, it has many to first. MGM Dizzee World was the first to bring the Jurong Bird Show of Singapore to India, a hot air balloon ride and in the year 2000, it was the first amusement park in India to give visitors pick-up and drop-in Helicopters. Apart from the water rides, land rides and the snow park, it has a stunning amphitheatre that hosts various events.

Timings: 10:00 AM – 06:00 PM

Price: Regular Ticket: Adult – INR 899, Child – INR 699

Address: No. 1/74, East Coast Road, Muttukadu, Chennai, Tamil Nadu

How to get there? It is situated alongside the east-coast road about 35km from Chennai. Public transport that is on the way to Mahabalipuram and Kovalam stops at this Water Park or you can simply book a cab.

Must try rides:Water coaster, a tornado, and a wave pool

Website: www.mgmdizzeeworld.com

Contact number: 044 2747 2129

Funtasia Island

Spread over 5 acres of land, the place has exhilarating and thrilling rides for both children and adults. It also consists of a wave pool, swimming pools, coffee shops, restaurants, a banquet hall, souvenir shops, and a lot more. The other attractions of Funtasia are the high-speed slides, spray guns, elephant houses and fun items for kids so they aren't left behind either.

Funcity

Funcity, Chandigarh spread over 43 acres of land is the biggest amusement park in the entire northern region. Housing 20 hydraulic rides like Octopus, Crazy Cars, Fun-slide, Big-wheel, Spin-o-spin, Froggy Fun, Crazy Bull, and Hedge Maze to name a few, the park gives its visitors excitement and much more fun. With six landing pools, the Wunder Water is a popular tourist attraction that also has one activity pool and a wave pool and 17 water slides with varied sizes and shapes. Apart from these, there are also Red Dragon slide, Crazy Cruise, Aqua Dance and a lot more to bring out your zest.

Timings: 10:00 AM – 06:00 PM

Price: Regular Ticket: Adult – INR 1300, Child – INR 1200

Address: Chandigarh-Nada Sahib Gurudwara Barwala Road, Near Ramgarh, NH - 72, Chandigarh, Punjab

How to get there? Funcity is located on Chandigarh-Nada Sahib Gurudwara Barwala Road, hence easily accessible. It is well connected to the city and can be visited by booking a private taxi or cab.

Must try rides: Octopus, Crazy Cars, Fun-slide, Big-wheel, Spin-o-spin, Froggy Fun, Crazy Bull, and Hedge Maze.

Website: www.funcitysurya.com
